São Paulo State - Brazil

São Paulo is the most populous and economically powerful state in Brazil. For Brazilians, it plays a role similar to what states like New York or California represent in the United States—a center of business, finance, innovation, and culture. Its capital city, also named São Paulo, is the largest city in Brazil and one of the biggest in the world. The state is known for its diverse population, strong industries, advanced infrastructure, and important agricultural production. It's a place where people from all over Brazil and the world come to work, study, and seek opportunity.
